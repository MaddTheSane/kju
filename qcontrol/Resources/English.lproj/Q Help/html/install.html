Q Help

Installing Q

  1. Grab the newest Build from http://www.kberg.ch/q
  2. Mount the .dmg
  3. Drag Q into the Applications Folder or to a place of your liking. That's it.

Removing Q

  1. Grab Q and drag it into the trashcan.
  2. If you want to remove the guest PCs, drag the Folder QEMU from your Documents folder to the trash.
  3. Q writes its prefferences to
    • ~/Library/ch.kberg.q.plist
    • ~/Library/ch.kberg.qemu.plist
    You can remove these, too.
